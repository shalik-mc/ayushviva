iCoffee

iCoffee is a functional coffee fortified with Salacia™ Extract, combining traditional Ayurvedic ingredients with modern nutritional science. It is formulated to support healthy blood sugar metabolism and overall metabolic wellness while letting you enjoy your daily cup of coffee.

Product Description

iCoffee is available in both Black Coffee and Creamer variants. Each serving combines premium coffee with carefully selected herbal extracts to support metabolic health, making it a convenient addition to a balanced lifestyle.

Composition – iCoffee Black (Per 10 g)

Ingredient Quantity
Coffee Arabica (Kaphi) 4000 mg
Kapikacchu (Mucuna pruriens) 500 mg
Saptaranga (Salacia spp.) 500 mg

Composition – iCoffee Creamer (Per 15 g Sachet)

Ingredient Quantity
Coffee Arabica (Kaphi) 3000 mg
Kapikacchu (Mucuna pruriens) 150 mg
Saptaranga (Salacia spp.) 150 mg

No Added Sugar

Directions for Use

  • Pour one sachet into approximately 120 ml of hot water.
  • Stir well and serve hot.
  • Consume up to two sachets daily or as advised by your healthcare practitioner.

Storage

  • Store in a cool and dry place.
  • Keep away from moisture and direct sunlight.

Wellness Benefits

  • Supports healthy blood sugar metabolism.
  • Helps maintain metabolic balance.
  • Supports healthy appetite regulation.
  • Complements healthy weight-management goals.
  • Supports cardiovascular wellness.
  • Helps maintain healthy cholesterol levels already within the normal range.
Important Note

iCoffee is a functional wellness beverage formulated with Ayurvedic ingredients. It is intended to support overall wellness and should not be used as a substitute for prescribed medications or medical treatment. Individuals with diabetes or other medical conditions should consult their healthcare professional before use.
