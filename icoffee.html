iCoffee

iCoffee is a premium instant coffee blend enriched with Ganoderma lucidum (Reishi mushroom), designed to combine the rich taste of coffee with selected botanical ingredients. It is marketed as a wellness beverage to complement a healthy lifestyle.

Key Features

  • Premium instant coffee blend
  • Contains Ganoderma lucidum (Reishi mushroom)
  • Rich, smooth coffee taste
  • Easy to prepare
  • Can be enjoyed hot or cold
  • Suitable for daily consumption as part of a balanced diet

Main Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee
  • Ganoderma lucidum (Reishi)
  • Coffee Extract
  • Natural Flavoring Ingredients

Suggested Use

Mix one sachet with approximately 150–200 ml of hot water. Stir well and enjoy. Follow the preparation instructions on the product packaging.
